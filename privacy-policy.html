My Bin Day - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

Overview

My Bin Day is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your information.

Data Collection

My Bin Day does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Location Information

  • Location access is used only to help you set your address for bin collection schedules
  • Location data is stored locally on your device only
  • No location information is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties

Bin Collection Data

  • The app retrieves publicly available bin collection schedules from local councils
  • This data is used solely to display collection information to you
  • No personal data is sent when requesting collection schedules

Data Storage

  • Your address and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

The app accesses public bin collection data from local council APIs. These services may have their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
